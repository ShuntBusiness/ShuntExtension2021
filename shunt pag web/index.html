Why are we doing this? What's our motivation?



It's a particular story. One of our members, when the fourth season of Money Heist (a series he was interested in seeing) came out, before he finished watching it they spoiled it, and it gave him a lot of anger. Together with another boy who is not in our project, he came up with the idea of ​​making an app for the EFSI 4th ​​grade subject in which spoilers would be avoided and that they cannot spoil you in any way unless it is in person (that that obviously can't be helped). That motivated us, and now it continues to motivate us that each person who is angry about being spoiled, no longer has to worry about it, and can watch a series, movie, or even play a video game with a quiet story and on time, without a page or social network spoiling you.


What's our vision?

Our vision is that Twitter, Whatsapp, Instagram, etc. be apps free of spoilers, that at least one company integrates us completely, and mainly that each person who is interested in watching a series, movie, or playing a video game with a story, uses our app to avoid having to face the need to cover your ears and eyes so as not to spoil yourself.
